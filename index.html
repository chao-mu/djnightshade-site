DJ Nightshade

About

Nightshade never met a broken beat she didn’t like; she started serving up her feel-good drum n bass in 1998, amidst a budding NYC bass culture. Since then, this esteemed lady of bass has performed at events coast to coast, including Warped Tour, Whistle, Candyball, Firefly, Elements, Transformus, Figment, and Burning Man. She has been guest featured on DNB Radio, Kind Radio, State of the Art, Bass Movement, and Radio Ninja.

Currently based in Boston Massachusetts, Nightshade continues to deliver her unique mix of dark rolling basslines, melodic grooves, and bone-crunching beats to the dancefloor carnivores.
